Project: MORTALIS

In ancient Greek religion, Metis was a mythical goddess, an Oceanid nymph, daughter of the Titans Oceanus and Tethys. She is the mother of wisdom and deep thought.

Metis, a Discord bot, is the culmination of my passion for the Discord API and my desire to create a tool that could assist with moderating our DnD-LMU server. With the help of my friend Daniel, we spent countless hours refining and expanding not only the bot but also our knowledge of Node.js, TypeScript, SQLite, and other web development tools. While there is still much to do and many ideas to persue, we're proud of what we've accomplished and are excited to share it with you.

Project: METIS

“Where words leave off, music begins.”
― Heinrich Heine

The DnD-Music-Player project was born out of the simple necessity and desire to improve handeling the music and creating atmospheres playing DnD. It was my first introduction into electron and therefore the first real industry standard tech I used. Like they sometimes do, this project needed to wait in the corner of my github profile for a few years before I decided it was time to give it another shot. Reworking almost everything, the current goal is to get to a working app with the necessary base functionalities. And then... who knows? I might get around to review the user interviews I did back then...
