Project: MORTALIS

In ancient Greek religion, Metis was a mythical goddess, an Oceanid nymph, daughter of the Titans Oceanus and Tethys. She is the mother of wisdom and deep thought.

Metis, a Discord bot, is the culmination of my passion for the Discord API and my desire to create a tool that could assist with moderating our DnD-LMU server. With the help of my friend Daniel, we spent countless hours refining and expanding not only the bot but also our knowledge of Node.js, TypeScript, SQLite, and other web development tools. While there is still much to do and many ideas to persue, we're proud of what we've accomplished and are excited to share it with you.
